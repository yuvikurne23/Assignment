FAQ

Professional Money Management & Research +
Mutual funds are managed by professional fund managers who regularly monitor market trends and economic trends for taking investment decisions. They also have dedicated research professionals working with them who make an in-depth study of the investment option to take an informed decision.
Risk Diversification +
Diversifying risk means spreading investments across different assets to reduce the impact of a single underperforming asset.
Convenience +
Mutual funds offer a hassle-free way to invest without needing to manage every stock or bond individually.
Reduction in Costs +
Mutual funds benefit from economies of scale, helping reduce trading costs and management expenses.
Tax Advantages +
Certain mutual funds provide tax-saving benefits under sections like 80C of the Income Tax Act.
Other Advantages +
Mutual funds also offer transparency, flexibility, and are regulated by SEBI, ensuring investor protection.
